A **loop** is a sequence of instructions repeated until a condition is met.

In {{Glossary("JavaScript")}}, common loops include {{jsxref("Statements/for", "for")}}, {{jsxref("Statements/while", "while")}}, and {{jsxref("Statements/do...while", "do...while")}}.
