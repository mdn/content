{{GlossarySidebar}}

A **boolean attribute** in ARIA is an {{Glossary("Enumerated", "enumerated attribute")}} that includes `true` or `false` in the enumerated list.

In ARIA, booleans only come in the form of strings `"true"` and `"false"`.
