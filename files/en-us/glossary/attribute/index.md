An **attribute** in {{Glossary("HTML")}} is a piece of markup that extends or modifies an {{Glossary("element")}}. Attributes are specified in the opening tag and usually take the form `name="value"`.

Common attributes include `class`, `id`, `src`, and `href`. Boolean attributes like `disabled` or `required` can be present or absent without requiring a value.
