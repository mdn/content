<p>Flexbox is the commonly-used name for the <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/css-flexbox-1/">CSS Flexible Box Layout Module</a>, a layout model for displaying items in a single dimension â€” as a row or as a column.</p>

<p>In the specification, Flexbox is described as a layout model for user interface design. The key feature of Flexbox is the fact that items in a flex layout can grow and shrink. Space can be assigned to the items themselves, or distributed between or around the items.</p>

<p>Flexbox also enables alignment of items on the main or cross axis, thus providing a high level of control over the size and alignment of a group of items.</p>
