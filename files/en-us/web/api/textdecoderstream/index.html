{{APIRef("Encoding API")}}

The TextDecoderStream interface of the {{domxref('Encoding API','','',' ')}} converts a stream of strings into bytes in the UTF-8 encoding. It is the streaming equivalent of {{domxref("TextDecoder")}}.
