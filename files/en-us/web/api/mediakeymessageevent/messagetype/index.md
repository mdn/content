<p>The <strong>MediaKeyMessageEvent.messageType</strong> read-only property indicates the
  type of message. It may be one of <code>license-request</code>,
  <code>license-renewal</code>, <code>license-release</code>, or
  <code>individualization-request</code>.</p>
