{{JSRef}}

The **`eraYear`** accessor property of {{jsxref("Temporal.PlainDate")}} instances returns a non-negative integer representing the year of this date within the era, or `undefined` if the calendar does not use eras (e.g. ISO 8601). The year index usually starts from 1 (more common) or 0, and years in an era can decrease with time (e.g. Gregorian BCE). `era` and `eraYear` together uniquely identify a year in a calendar, in the same way as `year` does.
