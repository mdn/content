{{JSRef}}

The **`dayOfWeek`** accessor property of {{jsxref("Temporal.PlainDate")}} instances returns a positive integer representing the 1-based day index in the week of this date. The first day of the week is `1`, and the last day of the week is the {{jsxref("Temporal/PlainDate/daysInWeek", "daysInWeek")}}. 1 usually represents Monday.
